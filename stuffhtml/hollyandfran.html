HOLLY AND FRAN

A GRAPHIC STORY

A story
about Holly Lynch and Fran Kaufman. Holly's family is killed, and to protect her, she is sent to live with her estranged godmother, Fran. This is the riveting tale about sacrifice, commitment, and the lengths one will go to for the person they love most.
