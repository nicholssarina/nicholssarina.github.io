ZYR
VS THE OTHER GUYS
A campaign
focused on pushing ZYR Vodka, an American vodka brand, onto a younger audience who may be disinterested in the high asking price for a bottle. Instead of focusing on quality and tasting points as other premium vodkas do, ZYR VS THE OTHER GUYS playfully pokes fun at the other vodka brands in an ambiguous yet propagandist tone. This campaign is full of print ads, an Instagram presence, and an interactive portion of Zyr's website to push the campaign's message.
