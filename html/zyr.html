ZYR
VS THE OTHER GUYS
A campaign
focused on pushing ZYR Vodka, an American vodka brand, onto a younger audience who may be disinterested in the high asking price for a bottle. Instead of focusing on quality and tasting points as other premium vodkas do, ZYR VS THE OTHER GUYS is a print campaign that playfully pokes fun at the other vodka brands in an ambiguous yet propagandist tone.
