About me

What is there to know? Well, I'm a music-loving, movie-watching aspiring creative. I'm always looking for new ways to express myself and bring a fresh perspective to everything I create. If you need me, I'm probably holed up in my room with my headphones on, noodling on my guitar, or recording an episode of my film podcast.

Once I make my way into the design world, my goal is to make people stop and take notice of what I've created, and hopefully inspire them to get creative themselves.
