About me

What is there to know? Well, I'm a music-loving, movie-watching, design-crazy creative with a whole lot of ambition. I'm always looking for new ways to express myself and bring a fresh perspective to everything I create. If you need me, I'm probably holed up in my room with my headphones on, doodling on my tablet or lost in a movie marathon.

Once I make my way into the design world, my goal is to make people stop and take notice of what I've created, and hopefully inspire them to get creative themselves.

So, if you need someone who's equal parts witty and creative, I'm your person. Let's make something great together!
