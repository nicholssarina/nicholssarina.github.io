About me

What is there to know? Well, I'm an aspiring creative who loves anything artistic and innovative. If I'm not designing, I'll likely be found with my eyes locked on a riveting film, my nose deep in an enthralling book, or headphones over my ears as I listen to spellbounding music. As a mass-consumer of the arts, I am always looking for new ways to express myself and bring a fresh perspective to everything I create.

As a designer/creative, my goal is to make people stop and take notice of what I've created, and hopefully inspire them to get creative themselves.
